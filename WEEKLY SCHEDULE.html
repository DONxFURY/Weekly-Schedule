Goodwill Mission

At Goodwill, we believe in the power of work to transform lives. Your dedication helps us fund job training programs and employment placement services for people in our community.

Goodwill Policies & Resources

Return Policy

Our return policy ensures customer satisfaction while supporting our mission:

  • Returns accepted within 14 days with original receipt
  • Items must be in original condition with tags attached
  • Electronics must be tested in-store before purchase
  • Final sale items (underwear, swimwear, etc.) cannot be returned
  • Returns for store credit only - no cash refunds
Donation Guidelines

Acceptable donation guidelines for our stores:

  • Clothing: Clean and in good condition
  • Furniture: No stains, tears, or broken parts
  • Electronics: Working condition (test before donating)
  • No mattresses or large appliances
  • No recalled or hazardous items
  • No weapons or flammable materials
Discount Policy

Employee discount and special sale days:

  • Employees receive 25% discount on all purchases
  • Discount applies to already discounted items
  • Color tag sales: 50% off specific color tags each day
  • Senior discount: 15% off every Wednesday
  • Military discount: 10% off with valid ID
Dress Code

Professional appearance requirements:

  • Goodwill-branded shirt (provided)
  • Clean, dark-colored pants or knee-length skirt
  • Closed-toe shoes (no sandals or flip-flops)
  • Name tag must be visible at all times
  • Minimal jewelry and visible tattoos must be covered
  • Hair must be neat and of natural colors
